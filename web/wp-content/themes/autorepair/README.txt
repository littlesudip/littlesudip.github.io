== Description ==

Auto Repair WordPress Theme is suitable for any kind of Auto Business like Auto Repair Services, Car Wash, Garages or Mechanical Workshops. This WordPress Theme can be customized to your specific needs with incredible ease, thanks to drag and drop PageLayer page builder( with alot of components ). It is easily customizable to meet your visual needs. Auto Repair is the best solution for all your auto service website needs. It has the customizing options like blog style, logo update, layout control, color modification, extra widgets, sticky menu and much more.
